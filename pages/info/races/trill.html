The Trill were a humanoid species native to the planet Trill. Some members of the species co-exist in a symbiotic relationship with a sentient organism known as a symbiont.

Trill were distinguished by a pattern of spots from the top of their head to their toes. These patterns differ from individual to individual however.

Those Trill that became joined to a Symbiont had personalities which were a synthesis of the two beings including the memories, and to some extent the personalities, of the previous hosts of the symbiont.

One of the most important parts of the joining process is the Zhin'tara ritual, where an unjoined Trill makes a connection between the current host and a friend or relative, temporarily placing the personality and memories of a previous host into the other. This is a way for hosts to gain closure, and is required by the Symbiosis Commission.
