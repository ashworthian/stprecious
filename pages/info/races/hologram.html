A hologram was a construction of photons, force fields, and holomatter created inside a holodeck, a holosuite, or by another type of holographic projector.

Holograms could be created to appear as locations, objects, animals or individuals, designed from holomatter, to have a life-like appearance and behavior.

Many holograms were not designed for long-term usage. If a hologram was running continuously, it was possible it could suffer from memory fragmentation.

In some cases, the computer program was sophisticated enough that a hologram could be considered self-aware or sentient.
