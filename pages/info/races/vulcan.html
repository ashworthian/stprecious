Vulcans (also known as "Vulcanians") are a humanoid species known for their devotion to logic, and stoic natures. They are native to the planet Vulcan.

Vulcans are also known for their pointed ears and their copper-based dark green colored blood. (traits they share with their Romulan cousins)

The Vulcans were the first alien species that Earth ever encountered. This meeting would lead to the formation of the United Earth Starfleet, the precursor to the Federation.

In 2161, their homeworld became a founding member of the United Federation of Planets.
