Humans (Homo sapiens) are the native sentient humanoid species of Earth. As a collective group, they are sometimes called humanity.

They were the only surviving race of several sentient/intelligent species to have evolved from the Genus Homo.

Humans were also one of two known spacefaring intelligent species to have originated from Earth; the other being the Voth, who had come to live in the Delta Quadrant by the 24th century.

Due to the warp flight test conducted by Zefram Cochrane in the year 2063, Earth was visited by the Vulcans. A meeting which would lead to the formation of the United Earth Starfleet and eventually the United Federation of Planets.
