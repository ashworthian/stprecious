The Fenrisal are a furred, lupine humanoid species from the planet Fenrisal. They were a prominant member of the Breen Confederacy whose snouted facial structure served as the inspiration for the archetypical Breen helmet design.

As of the year 2376, the Fenrisal have become independant of the Breen Confedaracy after fighting a civil war for said independance. Many Fenrisal would ultimately migrate to the Alpha Quadrant.

After migrating from the Gamma Quadrant, the Fenrisal applied to become a member of the United Federation of Planets of which they were approved.

Following their addition to the Federation, many Fenrisal who had previously fought in the Dominion War joined Starfleet. This would also include Matias, a Dominnion War veteran who would go on to serve aboard the USS Pureshashu.
