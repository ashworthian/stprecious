Caitians (Caiti sapiens) are the native sentient felinoid species of Cait, a class M world that orbits 15 Lyncis.

While the skin of some Caitians was covered with fur that varied in color from orange to black, other Caitians were without fur or trimmed down completely. Female Caitians grew long Human-like hair on their heads.

The voices females speak with had a soft, cat-like purring quality. Some Caitians did not find it necessary to use footwear.

As the Caitian homeworld was a Federation member, Caitians were known to have served in Starfleet and on the Federation Council, and were present as members in 2286.
