The Romulans (Rihan: Rihannsu, "the Declared"; singular "Rihanha") were a humanoid species from the planet Romulus. They were an off-shoot of the Vulcans, their ancestors having left Vulcan after rejecting the teachings of Surak.

The Romulan Star Empire was the Romulan polity and one of the major powers in the galaxy by the 24th century.

As of the year 2387, due to the Hobus Supernova that wiped out the Romulan system the Romulans have been on a slow climb of recovery with assistance provided by the Federation. However at some point the Romulans have mostly recovered from the Supernova that almost drove them to extinction.

In order to improve public relations, the Romulan Star Empire was reformed into The Romulan Republic which is the current Romulan polity in the 25th century and an ally of the Federation. However remnants of the former Romulan Star Empire still exist.
