The 25th century, defined in the Earth calendar as being from 2400 until 2499, was a period of expansion and great technological advancement for the Federation. It was also during this time period that the Alpha Quadrant and Beta Quadrant came under immense threat due to an invasion from the Undine, otherwise known as Species 8472. This invasion involved key representatives from the major powers being replaced by Undine operatives in order to incite chaos and war. A war that would enable the beings from Fluidic Space to take control. The Borg also make a return despite being thought made extinct in the 24th century.
