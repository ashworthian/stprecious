biography

pre-adult years

In 2366, Nathan was born to Admiral J'gred and Commander Tynisha as their first child. And from a very early age was intrigued by the mysteries of Space and the ideology of exploring it due to the stories his mother Tynisha would tell him about it when he was growing up. One such story involved how his parents first met.

In 2368, Nathan became a brother at the age of two when his mother gave birth to his first sister Ria. Tynisha explained to him when he was slightly older and could understand her words around the time his second sister Neomi was born in 2369 at the age of three that he was Ria and Neomi's big brother and needed to protect them from anything that might harm them.

Something he felt compelled to do since his father was extremely busy and was rarely able to visit them. This is the main reason that during the siblings later years both at home and at Starfleet Academy, the three relied on each other. So much that they would not allow any harm to come to one another.

When Nathan turned eighteen in 2384, he decided to finally follow in his parents footsteps and enroll to Starfleet Academy. It was only a few years prior to this that he also explained to Ria that she was Neomi's big sister and would need to take care of her since he would not be able to do so whilst being away from their home. Something which would become null and void when his two sisters joined Starfleet Academy as well.

pets

Nathan and by extension his family has owned many pets over the years, though only four in particular were specifically his. Three of these notable pets were cats named Tiger, Chester and Belle. The fourth notable pet which is his current pet is a Husky named Maverick. Maverick was given to Nathan by a friend of his mother's whose purebreed Huskies had given birth to a litter. Ever since Nathan has had Maverick, his father has been quite irritated since felines do not cope well with canines. But because of Nathan only being partially feline, canines have never bothered him.

chronology

  • 2366: Born on Luna to his parents, J'Gred and Tynisha.
  • 2368: Becomes the big brother to his first younger sister, Ria.
  • 2369: Becomes the older brother to his second younger sister, Neomi.
  • 2381: Visits his father's homeworld of Cait and bears witness to the Borg's demise.
  • 2384: Decides to follow in his parents' footsteps and enrolls in Starfleet.
  • 2387: Is inducted into the renewed Red Squad due to his stellar grades and extracurricular performances.
  • 2388: Graduates Starfleet Academy and is assigned to the Norway-class vessel, the USS Goliath.
  • 2389: Encounters the Undine Amirelye and in the process saves fellow officer Catherine Baxter. For his efforts he is promoted to Lieutenant Junior Grade.
  • 2391: Due to several years of exemplary service he is promoted to Lieutenant and reassigned to Deep Space Nine. He also meets Kestra Morganth, a new councillor aboard the station.
  • 2392: Begins dating Kestra as the two have become closer.
  • 2395: Is promoted again, this time to rank of Lieutenant Commander where he is reassigned to the Sovereign-class vessel, the USS Enterprise-E under the command of Captain Data.
  • 2396: Decides to end his relationship with Kestra as things had become strained due to maintaining a long distance relationship.
  • 2400: Is promoted to the rank of Commannder before being reassigned to the Prometheus-class vessel, the USS Redhawk as Executive Officer under his father's command.
  • 2407: Is temporary named Acting Captain of the Redhawk in his father's absence in order to test him for Captainhood.
  • 2408: Is once again promoted, this time to the rank of Captain. He then spends the rest of the year arranging ship assignments.
  • 2409: Takes command of the newly christined Vesta-class vessel, the USS Pureshashu. Later in this year, his ship encounters alternate timeline versions of Samantha, KOENA and Data.
