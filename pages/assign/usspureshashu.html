The USS Pureshasu (NCC-86521) was a Vesta-class Federation Starfleet vessel fulfilling missions for Starfleet during the early 25th century. The Pureshasu was launched in 2409, and her commanding officer was Captain Nathan Jenkins, with a crew of about 750.
